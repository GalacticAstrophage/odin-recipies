Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for pasta from inside a pasta box bought from the store.

Ingredients

  • One box of uncooked pasta
  • Some salt, (how much depends on how salty you want your pasta)
  • One medium sized pot
  • Water
  • One cullender

Steps

  1. Clear a clean work area and have a stove-top burner ready.
  2. Gather your ingredients on your working area.
  3. Fill your pot about 1/3 full
  4. Place your pot on the stove-top burner
  5. Turn on the stove-top burner based on what the box instructions say to do for different kinds of stoves
  6. Put salt in the pot, how much you add will effect how salty the pasta is
  7. Wait until the water in the pot boils
  8. Open the pasta box and put the uncooked pasta inside into the pot of boiling water
  9. Wait about 10 minuets
  10. Turn off the stove-top burner
  11. Wait for about one minute
  12. Put your cullender into your sink
  13. Pick up your pot of pasta and dump the contents of the pot into the cullender
  14. Place your pot on your workspace
  15. Pick the cullender full of cooked pasta up and dump its contents into the pot on your workspace
  16. Enjoy your pasta!
