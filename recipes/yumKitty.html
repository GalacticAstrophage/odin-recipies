YumKitty Snacks!

Description

This is a recipe for YumKitty snacks that your cat will absolutely despise. Just feed this to your cat if it was mean to you, and it will continue hating you for all eternity. (Don't actually feed this to your cat please, that's just mean.)

Ingredients

  • One freezer with available space
  • A lot of Water
  • One bowl
  • Dog hair (Idk why you would have this, but you need it for this recipe)
  • One spoon for stirring

Steps

  1. Grab your bowl and put it on a free workspace. (Preferably a kitchen counter or table)
  2. Fill your bowl half full with water.
  3. Sprinkle your dog hairs into the water.
  4. Stir the water slowly for 10 seconds.
  5. Put your bowl with water and dog hair into the freezer.
  6. Wait for the water to freeze.
  7. Take it out of the freezer
  8. Dig your spoon into the ice and chip away the ice to give to your annoyed cat!
  9. RUN AWAY IN TERROR TO AVOID GETTING BITTEN
