Yummy Air Recipe

Description

Everyone needs to breathe air. Some people like different kinds of air. This is a recipe that I hope some fellow humans will enjoy, (and maybe laugh at).

Ingredients

  • Two plastic zip-lock bags (To contain the air. Be sure to label the bags)
  • One bag-full of cold air
  • One bag-full of warm air
  • One pot/bowl
  • One spoon/stirring device

Steps

  1. Take the cold air bag, and dump it's contents into your bowl or pot.
  2. Take your stirring device and stir the air around a bit.
  3. Empty the warmer air into the bowl/pot.
  4. Stir the two air types around a bit more.
  5. Enjoy your breathable air!
